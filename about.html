About Us

Welcome to Career Craft, your trusted guide for career exploration and education planning after 10th & 12th grade. Our mission is to empower students to make informed decisions about their future by providing comprehensive resources, expert guidance, and personalized support.

Our Story

We understand the challenges students face when choosing the right courses and colleges after 12th grade or 10th grade. We come together to create a platform that simplifies this process and helps students achieve their goals.

What We Offer

  • Career Assessments: Discover your strengths and interests with our interactive assessments.
  • Course and College Finder: Explore a vast database of courses and colleges tailored to your needs.
  • Resources and Tips: Access a wealth of information on career development, education, and more.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help students navigate the complex world of career choices and education planning, ensuring they make informed decisions that align with their passions and goals.
